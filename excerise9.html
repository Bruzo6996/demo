Chicken biriyani

Biryani is a popular and flavorful dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is a mixed rice dish made with fragrant basmati rice, spices, and usually includes meat (such as chicken, mutton, beef, or fish) or vegetables. Biryani is known for its aromatic and rich taste, achieved through the layering of cooked rice and marinated meat or vegetables along with a blend of spices.

Paneer Biryani is a delicious vegetarian variation of the traditional biryani, where marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) is used as the main protein instead of meat. It is a flavorful and aromatic dish that combines fragrant basmati rice, soft and succulent paneer cubes, and a blend of spices to create a satisfying meal.
