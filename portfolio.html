Captain America








About

Captain America, also known as Steve Rogers, is a fictional superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. Created by writer Joe Simon and artist Jack Kirby, the character first appeared in Captain America Comics #1 in 1941 during the World War II era. Steve Rogers is a frail young man who undergoes a super-soldier experiment that enhances his strength, agility, and endurance to the peak of human potential. He fights for justice, freedom, and the American way as the leader of the Avengers.
